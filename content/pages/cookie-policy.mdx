## Introduction

This Cookie Policy explains how Brow Tint uses cookies and similar technologies on [tintbrow.com](https://tintbrow.com). It should be read alongside our [Privacy Policy](/privacy-policy).

## What is a cookie?

A cookie is a small text file stored on your device by your browser when you visit a website. Cookies allow a site to remember your actions and preferences (such as login state, language, or consent) over time, so you don't have to re-enter them on every visit.

## How we use cookies

We use cookies for two purposes:

1. **Strictly necessary** — required for the site to function. Examples: keeping you signed in, remembering your locale, recording your cookie consent choice. These cannot be disabled if you want to use Brow Tint.
2. **Analytics** — help us understand how the site is used so we can improve it. Examples: anonymous page-view counts and aggregated session data via privacy-first analytics tooling (when configured). These are loaded only after you accept cookies.

We do **not** use third-party advertising cookies or cross-site tracking cookies on tintbrow.com.

## Cookies we set

| Cookie | Purpose | Lifetime |
|--------|---------|----------|
| `cookie-consent` | Records your accept / reject choice for the cookie banner | 1 year |
| Session / auth cookies | Keep you signed in across page loads | Session or up to 30 days |
| Locale cookie | Remembers your language choice | Up to 1 year |

If analytics or advertising integrations are enabled by the site operator, additional cookies may be set by those providers; details would appear here when applicable.

## Your choices

When you first visit Brow Tint, the cookie banner lets you:

- **Accept all** — strictly-necessary + analytics cookies are stored.
- **Reject non-essential** — only strictly-necessary cookies are stored; analytics are blocked.

You can change your mind at any time by clearing the `cookie-consent` cookie in your browser, which will re-show the banner on your next visit. You can also block or delete cookies entirely through your browser settings; some site features may not work as a result.

## Updates

We may update this Cookie Policy when we change how cookies are used. The `created_at` date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision.
