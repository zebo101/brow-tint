## Introduction

Welcome to Brow Tint, an AI-powered platform designed to help you preview and explore different brow tint looks using advanced artificial intelligence technology. At Brow Tint, your privacy is of utmost importance to us, and this privacy policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our services.

## Information Collection and Use

We collect the following types of data to provide you with a better experience while using Brow Tint:

1. **Account Information**
   - **What We Collect**: This includes your name, email address, and any other information you provide when creating an account.
   - **Purpose**: To manage your account and provide customer support.

2. **Usage Details**
   - **What We Collect**: Information about how you use Brow Tint, including your interactions, features accessed, and usage frequency.
   - **Purpose**: To analyze user engagement and improve our services.

3. **Device Information**
   - **What We Collect**: Data regarding the devices you use to access Brow Tint, such as device type, operating system, and browser type.
   - **Purpose**: To optimize our services for different devices and ensure compatibility.

4. **Cookies**
   - **What We Collect**: Small data files placed on your device that help us track user preferences and improve user experience.
   - **Purpose**: To enhance the functionality of our services and personalize your experience.

5. **Payment and Billing Information**
   - **What We Collect**: Information regarding your payment methods, such as credit card number, billing address, and any details required for transaction processing.
   - **Purpose**: To facilitate billing and payment processing for our services.

## Photo Uploads & Biometric Data

Brow Tint is an image-generation product. When you use the AI Brow Tint Studio, you may upload one or more photos of yourself or another person. Because these images contain a face, they may qualify as **biometric data** under regulations such as the EU/UK GDPR, which we treat as a special category requiring extra care.

- **What we collect**: Photos you actively upload through the Studio. We do not extract or store separate biometric templates (e.g. face vectors), and we do not perform identification or matching on your photos.
- **Why**: To render the brow tint preview you requested, and to deliver the resulting image back to you.
- **Who processes it**: Photos are sent to our AI model partners — currently **fal.ai**, **kie.ai**, and **Replicate** — purely to compute the requested preview. These partners are bound by their own privacy terms and process the image only for the duration of the request.
- **Retention**: Uploaded source photos are deleted from our active processing systems within **24 hours** of generation. Generated previews remain in your account history until you delete them or close your account.
- **Not used for training**: Your photos are not used to train any model — neither ours nor our AI partners'.
- **Deletion on request**: You can delete any photo or generated image from your account at any time, and you can request deletion of your account and all associated images by contacting us at the address below.
- **Consent for third parties**: Only upload photos of people who have given you permission. Do not upload photos of minors without a parent or guardian's consent.

## Your Rights

If you are in the European Economic Area (EEA), the United Kingdom, or another jurisdiction that grants similar protections, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

- **Access** — request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
- **Rectification** — ask us to correct inaccurate or incomplete data.
- **Erasure** ("right to be forgotten") — request deletion of your data, including your account and uploaded photos.
- **Portability** — receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format, and have it transmitted to another controller where technically feasible.
- **Restriction** — ask us to limit how we use your data while a request is being resolved.
- **Objection** — object to processing of your data for direct marketing or other purposes.
- **Withdraw consent** — where processing is based on consent, you may withdraw it at any time without affecting the lawfulness of processing before the withdrawal.
- **Lodge a complaint** — file a complaint with your local data protection supervisory authority.

To exercise any of these rights, email **[privacy@tintbrow.com](mailto:privacy@tintbrow.com)**. We will respond within 30 days. If we cannot identify you from the request, we may ask for additional information to verify your identity before acting.
