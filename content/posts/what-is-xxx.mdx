Brow Tint is the AI brow tint generator that turns one front-facing photo into a full lookbook of professional-looking brow tints. Instead of guessing how a shade will read on your face — or booking a salon appointment to find out — you upload, pick a look, and watch the side-by-side preview render in seconds.

## How it works

1. **Upload a clear front-facing photo.** Soft lighting, brows fully visible, no glasses. Your image is used only to generate the preview and is auto-deleted shortly after.
2. **Pick a brow look.** Browse curated styles — soft taupe, espresso, brow lamination, lash & brow combo — or filter by shade, shape, and intensity.
3. **Compare before & after.** Drag the divider, regenerate variations, and download the result to bring to your brow artist.

## What you can preview

- **Tint shades** from soft taupe through deep espresso
- **Brow lamination + tint** for sculpted, brushed-up brows
- **Tinted brow gel** day-to-day looks
- **Lash & brow tint** combinations for full eye framing
- **Brow code tint** style matches for warm, neutral, or cool undertones

## Why use it

- **Avoid mistakes.** See the result before committing to a shade or formula.
- **Communicate clearly.** Show your brow artist exactly the look you want.
- **Save time.** Try ten styles in the time a salon consultation takes for one.
- **Privacy first.** Photos are processed for your preview only and not used to train models.
