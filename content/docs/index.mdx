## What is Brow Tint?

Brow Tint is an AI-powered brow tint generator. Upload a front-facing photo, pick a brow look, and see a side-by-side preview in seconds — useful for finding the right tint shade before you visit a brow artist or salon.

## Photo guidelines

For the best result, your photo should be:

- Front-facing, with both brows clearly visible
- In soft, even lighting (avoid hard shadows on the brow line)
- Without glasses, hats, or hair covering the brow
- JPG or PNG, up to 10 MB

## Privacy

Photos you upload are used only to render the preview you requested. They are processed via our AI partners (fal, kie, replicate) and auto-deleted shortly after generation. We do not use your photos to train models.

For details, see the [Privacy Policy](/privacy-policy).
